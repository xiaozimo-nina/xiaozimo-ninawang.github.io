Myopia Eyesight abnormality resulting from the eye's faulty refractive ability; distant objects appear blurred.
Spinal disease Spinal disease refers to a condition impairing the backbone. These include various diseases of the back or spine, such as kyphosis. Dorsalgia refers to back pain.
Obesity Obesity is a complex disease involving an excessive amount of body fat. Obesity isn't just a cosmetic concern. It is a medical problem that increases your risk of other diseases and health problems, such as heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure and certain cancers.

Divices' name Evaluation
Phone More than 5 billion people own mobile phones, and over half of those are smartphones.

According to a survey conducted in February 2021, nearly half of the respondents stated that on average they spent five to six hours on their phone on a daily basis, not including work-related smartphone use. A further 22 percent of respondents said that they spent three to four hours on average on their phone daily.
Laptop As of February 2019, 74 percent of Americans owned a desktop or laptop computer.

Laptop penetration rates are above 47 percent across each of the main age groups, and highest among those aged 35-54 years.
Desktop 3 Billion People Worldwide Are Gamers, and Nearly Half Play on PCs. A new report claims that 48 percent of all gaming happens on PCs, with a whopping 3.1B gamers worldwide across all platforms, consoles, and mobile devices.

Video gamers spent about eight hours and 27 minutes each week playing games, which is an increase of 14% over 2020, according to a new worldwide survey. Limelight Networks also found that 44% play more than seven hours per week and 25% clock more than 12 hours per week.
Divices' name How to minimise the health risks
Phone Try to find some books to read instead of using too much time to play on your phone. And when you are on the way to home or work, you can listen to some light music or find someone to chat with you, please don't just watch your phone. Also, when you are staying with your family or friends, please care about them more, and don't just find a "peace" place to play on your phone.
Laptop I admit you need it when you're in the office, but please increase your productivity and take time to relax your mind, eyes and muscles during your busy work hours. In your free time, go out and make friends with nature.
Desktop Speaking of desktop computers, many people think of computer games at the first time. Yes playing games can make us feel relaxed and happy, but we should have a limited time to play it every day. What's more, when playing games, we should make sure that we have a comfortable environment. After playing for a while, we should stop. It will help you protect your eyes.

Conclusion: The development of science and technology has both advantages and disadvantages. We should learn to control the extent of using science and technology by ourselves and use science and technology without causing laziness and disease.

The use of computers may give us some diseases, we should learn effective ways to prevent these diseases, at the same time, if we are unfortunate to catch these diseases, we should also learn simple treatment to prevent their further deterioration.
